About EagleReach

EagleReach is a public, open‑source civic assistant that connects U.S. residents with elected officials, voter information, city service updates (where available), weather forecasts, and roadwork/traffic context — all by ZIP code. The mission is to make civic access fast, trustworthy, and free for everyone.

“Government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the Earth.” — Abraham Lincoln

Benefits: one‑stop access to your representatives; authoritative voter links; quick actions to raise local concerns; and a privacy‑respecting, ad‑free experience built entirely on open data.
