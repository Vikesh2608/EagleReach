About EagleReach

EagleReach is a public, open‑source civic assistant for U.S. residents. One ZIP code unlocks what people need right now—your elected representatives with official links and phone numbers, voter information and education, election dates, weather, city updates, traffic, and trustworthy resources.

Why this exists. Democracy works best when access is simple. Civic information is often scattered, gated, or hard to read. EagleReach brings it together—cleanly, quickly, and without tracking—so kids, students, working professionals, elders, and officials can make better decisions together.

  • 100% open data, no sign‑in, no paywalls, no tracking.
  • Frictionless UX: one place, one search, human‑readable results.
  • Trustworthy sources (GovTrack, state election sites, Open‑Meteo, city open data).
  • Accessibility first: large touch targets, keyboard friendly, mobile‑ready.
  • Extensible: designed to add new states, cities, and services without vendor lock‑in.

Vision. To make civic participation as easy as checking the weather, so every resident can be informed, every official can be reachable, and every community can move forward—together.
