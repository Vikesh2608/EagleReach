About EagleReach

EagleReach is a public, open‑source civic assistant that connects U.S. residents with elected officials, weather forecasts, traffic/roadworks, city service updates (where available), and voter information — all by ZIP code. The goal is to make civic access fast, trustworthy, and free for everyone.
