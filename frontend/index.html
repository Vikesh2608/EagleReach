About Eagle Reach

Eagle Reach connects neighbors to trustworthy civic information—fast. We blend open data and public sources to show your elected officials, contacts, and upcoming elections, then surface local and global news so you can stay informed. Our goal is simple: help people participate, speak up, and look out for each other. An informed community is a safer, stronger community.

Vikesh Sagar Bairam holds a Master’s from the University of Illinois; a Bachelor of Computer Applications from the University of Allahabad; a Bachelor of Psychology from Osmania University; and an Advanced Diploma of Supply Chain Management from Alison (Brisbane, Australia).
